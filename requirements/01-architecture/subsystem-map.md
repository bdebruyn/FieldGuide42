## Subsystem Approval Criteria

A subsystem may be approved when it has at least one of the following:

- Owns data.
- Owns business rules.
- Owns security rules.
- Owns a distinct lifecycle.
- Owns an external integration.
- Has distinct scaling or performance requirements.
- Has a clear reason to isolate it from other responsibilities.
